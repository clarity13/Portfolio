A little about myself

Hi my full name is Jonathan Sang but my friends call me Jon and my family refers to me as JJ. I was born and raised in New York with a passion for creating, in whatever that may be. Being able to make a humans life better whether it be in real life or in UX is one of the great joys in life. In my past life I was in Accounting and it never really satisfied my cravings for creating and so I quit my job and decided to take the leap into studiying UX Design. It wasn’t easy decision to make because I was scared at the time but I’m so glad I did. By doing this I now understood who I wanted to be and what I wanted to do in life.

When I’m not being creative I love to play video games still as I came from playing esports competitively in the past. I go to the gym to always be a better version of myself. Travel and exploring is always a must to expands ones horizons and to keep oneself humble.
