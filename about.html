The Problem

Grocery shopping has generally three types of people in the world. Shoppers who are impatient, shoppering who use coupons to save money, and the third shopper who doesn’t really care. I know that most of the time people are the third shopper because it is always great to save time and money. During my research I didn’t find many applications that make shopping easier except Sams Club. The more advanced version of this application is Amazons grocery store which is extremely technology based. Shopping in stores need to get with the times and needs to innovate. As of right now it isn’t as seamless as it could be.
